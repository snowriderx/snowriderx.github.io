Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Slice Master free to play?
A: Yes, Slice Master is completely free to play in your browser with no downloads required.

Q: Can I play on mobile devices?
A: Absolutely! Slice Master is optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers.

Q: How do I unlock new knives?
A: Progress through levels and achieve high scores to unlock legendary knives with unique effects.

Q: Why isn't the game loading?
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or ensuring JavaScript is enabled.
